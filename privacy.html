Summary in plain English
Brain Checker collects your email and name to create your account. The PDF reports you upload are processed in memory to generate AI answers — they are never stored as files on our servers. We store text embeddings (mathematical representations of your reports) in a secure database so the AI can answer your questions. We do not sell your data. We do not show you ads.

1 Who we are

Bishop is an AI-powered post-counseling assistant operated by Brain Checker ("Brain Checker", "we", "us", or "our"), a career counseling organisation based in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Bishop platform at bishop.brainchecker.in (or any Render-hosted URL we provide).

By creating an account and using Bishop, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

2 What information we collect

Account information (provided by you at signup):

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a secure hash by Supabase — we never see your plain-text password)

Usage information (generated automatically):

  • Which Brain Checker product you selected (e.g. DMIT, Psychometric)
  • Number of questions asked per month (for usage limit enforcement)
  • Which analysis cards you used and when
  • Chat messages you send and the AI responses you receive

PDF report content (processed temporarily):

When you upload a PDF report, we extract its text, split it into sections, and generate mathematical embeddings. See Section 3 for full details.

We do NOT collect:

  • Payment or banking information (no payments are processed through Bishop)
  • Device location or GPS data
  • Biometric data
  • Fingerprint scan data (DMIT raw fingerprint images are handled only by Brain Checker counselors — not by this software)

3 How your PDF reports are handled

Important: Your PDF files are NEVER stored on our servers or in our database as files.

Here is exactly what happens when you upload a PDF report to Bishop:

  1. The PDF file bytes are received by our backend server running on Render.com.
  2. Text is extracted from the PDF entirely in memory — it is never written to any disk or file system.
  3. The extracted text is split into sections and converted into numerical vectors called embeddings (mathematical representations of meaning — not readable text).
  4. These embeddings, along with small text excerpts, are stored in our Supabase database linked to your session.
  5. When you ask a question, the AI uses these embeddings to retrieve the most relevant sections of your report and generate an answer.
  6. When you log out, your session data (embeddings and text excerpts) is deleted from the database.

The original PDF file is discarded from server memory as soon as step 4 is complete. It is not cached, backed up, or accessible after that point.

Confidentiality of report content: Report text excerpts stored in the database are associated with your account only. No other user can access your report data. Brain Checker staff do not routinely access report content but may do so for technical troubleshooting with your explicit consent.

4 How we use your information

  • To provide the service: Authenticate your account, load your product and session, process your PDFs, and generate AI answers.
  • To enforce usage limits: Track monthly question usage to apply the fair-use limit included with your Brain Checker counselling package.
  • To improve the service: Aggregate, anonymised usage patterns (e.g. which cards are most used) help us improve prompts and features. We do not use individual chat content for AI training.
  • To provide support: If you contact us with a problem, we may need to look at your account information to help you.
  • To communicate with you: We may send account-related emails (e.g. verification, password reset) via Supabase Auth. We do not send marketing emails without your consent.

5 Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate Bishop:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Database, authentication, and vector storage. Your account data and report embeddings are stored on Supabase servers. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR-compliant. Data is stored in AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai, India) when the India-region project is selected, or in the EU/US depending on your project region.
  • OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) — AI inference gateway. Your chat messages and relevant report excerpts are sent to OpenRouter to generate responses. OpenRouter routes requests to the configured language model (e.g. GPT-4o). OpenRouter's privacy policy applies to this data in transit. We do not share your name or email with OpenRouter.
  • Render (render.com) — Cloud hosting for the backend server. Render processes requests but does not store your data beyond standard server logs (retained for 7 days on the free tier).

We may disclose your information if required by Indian law, a court order, or other lawful authority, or to protect the safety of our users.

6 Data storage and security

  • Account data (email, name, usage counts) is stored in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) — meaning your data is only accessible to your own account.
  • All communication between your browser, our server, and Supabase is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt by Supabase Auth — we never see or store your plain-text password.
  • JWT tokens used for authentication expire and are stored only in your browser's sessionStorage (not cookies), reducing cross-site request forgery risk.
  • Report embeddings are stored only for the duration of your session and are deleted on logout.

Despite these measures, no internet service is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your Bishop account.

7 Data retention

  • Report embeddings and chat history: Deleted when you log out (or when you manually clear your session from within the app).
  • Account information (email, name): Retained until you request account deletion.
  • Usage logs: Retained for 12 months for service improvement, then deleted.
  • Server logs (Render): Retained for 7 days by Render, then automatically deleted.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at support@brainchecker.in with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

8 Your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (India) and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data (e.g. wrong name on your account).
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and all personal data.
  • Grievance redressal: Raise a complaint about how we have handled your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@brainchecker.in. We will respond within 30 days.

9 Cookies and tracking

Bishop does not use cookies for authentication or session management. Your login token is stored in sessionStorage, which is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any advertising trackers. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy and use a privacy-first, cookie-free solution (such as Plausible or Umami).

Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN for typography. Google may log your IP address when serving these fonts. To avoid this, you can use a browser extension that blocks Google Fonts requests.

10 Children's privacy

Bishop is used by Brain Checker counselors and the parents or guardians of students — not by children directly. If a student is under 18, their parent or guardian must create the account and use the service on their behalf.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account without parental consent, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
